# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

Relay44 expects a respectful, professional, and evidence-driven community. This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, release threads, and direct project communication.

## Expected Behavior

- communicate with respect, even when you disagree
- keep feedback technical, specific, and actionable
- assume good faith until there is evidence otherwise
- focus on the change, the behavior, or the risk instead of the person
- respect project boundaries around security, private infrastructure, and responsible disclosure

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, threats, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- personal attacks, intimidation, or repeated bad-faith engagement
- publishing private or security-sensitive information without permission
- deliberate disruption of triage, review, or release processes
- impersonation or misrepresentation of project authority

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- release and security coordination initiated through the project
- maintainer and contributor interactions in project-managed channels

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when conduct rules are violated.

Responses may include:

- a warning
- temporary moderation or limited participation
- permanent removal from project-managed spaces

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `support@relay44.com`.

Include:

- a short description of the incident
- links, screenshots, or message excerpts if available
- any immediate safety concern or urgency

Reports are handled confidentially where possible. Maintainers may disclose the minimum information required to investigate or protect project participants.
